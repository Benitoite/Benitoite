Quick help

How do I capture stable readings at low notes?
Ensure your device mic isn’t obstructed, reduce room noise. Low-note assist is built-in. For pianos, hold notes a bit longer for stable detection.
The app crashes when I close the window (macOS)
Please update to the latest version. If it persists, email support with your macOS version and steps to reproduce.
Does Octave Scope work offline?
Yes. All processing is on-device. Support email works via your mail app.

Privacy
